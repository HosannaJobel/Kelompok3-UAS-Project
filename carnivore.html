CARNIVORE

Tiger

Panthera tigris

Tigers are distributed across Asia, from the tropical rainforests of Indonesia to the boreal forests of Russia. Subspecies of tigers, such as the Sumatran tiger, live on the Indonesian island of Sumatra.

Leopard

Panthera pardus

Leopards have a wide distribution in sub-Saharan Africa and parts of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. They inhabit a variety of habitats, ranging from tropical rainforests to savannas.

Cheetah

Acinonyx jubatus

Cheetahs are mainly found in the grasslands and savannas of Africa, with a small population in Iran. They prefer open habitats that allow them to utilize their high speed when hunting.

Lion

Panthera leo

Lions mainly live in the grasslands and savannas of sub-Saharan Africa. There is also a small population of Asiatic lions found in the Gir Forest, India.

Eagle

Haliaeetus leucocephalus / Aquila chrysaetos

Eagles are distributed across the world, inhabiting habitats ranging from mountains, and forests, to coastal areas, depending on the species.

Crocodile

Crocodylus porosus / Crocodylus niloticus

Crocodiles are found in tropical regions of Africa, Asia, the Americas, and Australia, usually in freshwater habitats such as rivers, lakes, swamps, and wetlands.

Shark

Carcharodon carcharias / Sphyrna mokarran

Sharks inhabit almost all of the world's oceans, from shallow waters near the coast to the deep ocean, depending on the species.

Komodo Dragon

Varanus komodoensis

Komodo dragons are endemic to Indonesia, and found on the islands of Komodo, Rinca, Flores, Gili Motang, and Gili Dasami. They live in tropical savanna and monsoon forest habitats.

Wolf

Canis lupus

Wolves have a wide distribution in the northern hemisphere, including North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. They inhabit a variety of habitats, ranging from tundra, forests, and grasslands, to mountains.
